Fast Paced Black Jack

RULES/Directions:

Press "Start Game" to have both you and the Dealer start with two cards. You can only "Hit" or "Stand" once, so gamble your win. If Dealer has 17, over 21, or you "stand" and you have a higher total, than "You win". If You have 21 or BlackJack, BUT the Dealer has BlackJack, than you lose. If both you and the Dealer have the same total, then you "draw". GOOD LUCK!
